JustAR, We have been innovating AR since 2020

Augmented Reality technology has revolutionized how we interact with digital content in the real world. At Justupmind, we harness this power to create meaningful experiences that bridge the gap between physical and virtual environments.

Since our founding in 2020, we've been at the forefront of AR innovation, developing cutting-edge solutions for education, entertainment, and business applications. Our team of passionate AR experts combines technical excellence with creative vision to deliver exceptional results.

Our Mission & Vision

Our Mission

We are committed to making volumetric 3D capture accessible and affordable worldwide. Our purpose is to empower everyone to capture, process, and share their 3D visions seamlessly, fostering creativity and innovation across industries.

Our Vision

We envision a world where advanced volumetric 3D technology is within everyone’s reach, empowering creators, educators, and enterprises to transform ideas into immersive 3D experiences.
